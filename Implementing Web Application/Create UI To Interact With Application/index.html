Paper Tubes

Paper Tubes are wood pulp products, also known as cardboard tubes wound into cylindrical shapes for a wide variety of functions in different industries.Paper tubes are manufactured from recycled paper and can be used over again, making them a cost-efficient alternative to metal, plastic, glass and wood.Paper tubes are comparably quite cost-effective because they are easier for manufacturers to cut, purchase and dispose of than most other materials. Most commonly used for transporting papers, posters and other roll-able items, shipping tubes and mailing tubes are widely used by large companies and individuals alike.
